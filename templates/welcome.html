Welcome to the JTBD Interview Simulator

The JTBD Interview Simulator is your training ground to refine and master interviewing skills, particularly when interacting with high-level executives. Built on the renowned Jobs-To-Be-Done (JTBD) framework, this tool will help you uncover actionable insights into business needs and customer motivations.

The simulator offers a dynamic experience where you can:

  • Choose a specific business domain to focus your interview.
  • Engage with simulated executive personas who respond in real-time.
  • Receive feedback and evaluation based on your performance.

You can select between two distinct interview modes:

  • Full Interview Mode: Designed for comprehensive assessment, this mode allows you to conduct a complete, focused interview. It provides natural conversation flow with a maximum of 30 questions, followed by detailed post-interview analysis and feedback.
  • Guided Interview Mode: Ideal for learning and practice, this mode provides real-time guidance and evaluation after every few questions. It has no limit on the number of questions and ensures interactive feedback to enhance your skills throughout the session.

Whether you are preparing for client meetings, conducting customer interviews, or improving your general communication skills, the JTBD Interview Simulator is here to support you every step of the way.
