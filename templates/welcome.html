Welcome to the JTBD Interview Simulator

Refine Your Interviewing Skills

The JTBD Interview Simulator is designed to help you refine your interviewing skills based on the Jobs-To-Be-Done (JTBD) framework. This framework, developed by Christensen, Moesta, and Klement, focuses on understanding customer needs and behaviors by uncovering the "jobs" they hire products or services to fulfill.

In this application, you will:

  • Choose a business domain and interview a simulated executive persona.
  • Receive real-time evaluations of your interviewing skills.
  • Get actionable feedback and a comprehensive analysis report.

Use this simulator to enhance your ability to conduct meaningful interviews, discover customer insights, and make informed decisions. Click "Start the Interview" below to begin!
